About ColorStack at UNC Charlotte

Our Mission

ColorStack at UNC Charlotte is dedicated to empowering and supporting students of color in computer science and technology fields.

Our Goals

  • Foster a supportive community for students of color in tech
  • Provide mentorship and networking opportunities
  • Organize skill-building workshops and events
  • Connect students with internship and career opportunities
